For example, the Regular Member could use the posting function to become a potential buyer looking for other co-owners to join in the purchase of property together or the Regular Member could be an existing property owner who is looking to sell a part or a fraction of his/her ownership in a property or asset.

Regular Membership is available to everybody. The members will be free to list properties to sell, to buy properties, to charter or rent a property, to post an entry to find other co-owners, and to join others to co-own a property or asset. The listing of the properties or assets on WePropertyowners site is free and they could then post it on WeCoOwn site to make the listing visible to all users of the platform.

This Regular Membership cost will always be free.

2. Premium Membership is intended for a higher level use of the platform, the web, and mobile applications by the owners and co-owners to manage their properties. In addition to the privileges that Regular Members have, they will have access to the expanded use of the co-ownership Property Booking & Management System (PBMS) to manage the properties that they own on an on-going monthly subscription basis.

Premium Members will be able to view all listings on the WePropertyowners platform. In addition, they will also enjoy the premium promotional exposure of a fixed number of their property or asset listings on WePropertyowners on a monthly basis so that their premium listings will be visible to all visitors of the WePropertyowners site.

3. Professional Membership is designed for corporate accounts, licensed brokers, dealers, agents, and professional management companies who conduct either fractional share programs or time share programs for their own clients.

Professional Members will have all the privileges that Premium Members have but they will have unlimited promotional property or asset listings on the WePropertyowners site.

Professional Members may also subscribe to an additional White Label Membership to set up a white-labeled PBMS software app for their own retail clients. For more info on this program please send your inquiry to info@wecoown.com.
