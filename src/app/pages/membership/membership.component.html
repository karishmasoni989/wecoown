Basic Membership is available to everybody. The members will be free to list properties to sell, to buy properties, to charter or rent a property, to list to find other co-owners and to join others to co-own a property. The cost is free.

2. Basic Plus Membership is intended for a higher level use of the platform and the web applications by the owners and co-owners to manage their properties.

The number of listings are limited to 10 listings. The cost is $29 per month on a pre-paid month to month basis. The subscription could be cancelled before the end of the any month but there is no refund for the remaining prorated period of that month.

3. Premium Membership is intended for a higher level use of the platform and the web applications by the owners and co-owners to manage their properties. In addition to the privileges that Basic Plus Members have, they will have access to the expanded use of the co-ownership property management web applications to manage the properties that they own on an on-going monthly basis.

The number of listings are limited to 50 listings. The cost is $99 per month on a pre-paid month to month basis. The subscription could be cancelled before the end of the any month but there is no refund for the remaining prorated period of that month.

4. Professional Membership is designed for corporate accounts, licensed brokers, dealers, agents and professional management companies who conduct either fractional share programs or time share programs for their own clients.

Professional Members will have almost unlimited property listings (500). The cost is $499 per month on a pre-paid month to month basis. The subscription could be cancelled before the end of the any month but there is no refund for the remaining prorated period of that month.

Professional Members may also subscribe to set up a white-labeled app for their own clients. For more info on this program please send your inquiry to info@wecoown.com.
